## Code of Conduct

This project follows the 
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience
for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**
* Using welcoming and inclusive language
* Being respectful of differing viewpoints
* Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**
* Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
* Personal or political attacks
* Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of 
acceptable behavior.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project 
maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and 
fairly.
